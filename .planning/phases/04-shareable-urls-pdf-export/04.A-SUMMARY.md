## Decisions Made

1. **Dual router pattern**: Split share.py into `share_router` (authenticated, mounted at /api/v1/analyses) and `public_share_router` (unauthenticated, mounted at /s). Cleanest approach from the plan options.
2. **Allow-list sanitization**: sanitize_analysis_for_share uses explicit field inclusion rather than exclusion, preventing accidental PII exposure if new fields are added to the Analysis model.
